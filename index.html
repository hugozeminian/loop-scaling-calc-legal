Privacy Policy — 4-20mA Loop & PLC Calc

Effective date: 2026-06-17 · Version: v1 (app 0.1.0)

4-20mA Loop & PLC Calc (“the app”) is a local-first utility. This policy describes how the app handles data in version 1.

Summary

The app collects no personal data. It requires no account and has no backend. Everything runs on your device, offline.

Data we collect

None. The app does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal information.

Data stored on your device

The app saves a small amount of non-personal preference data locally so it remembers your choices:

  • last-used direction (mA→EU or EU→mA) for the loop tab
  • last-used direction (Raw→EU or EU→Raw) for the PLC tab
  • last-used range inputs (LRV, URV, unit label) for the loop tab
  • last-used range inputs (count range, EU range, unit label, bit preset) for the PLC tab
  • saved loop presets (name, LRV, URV, unit label)
  • saved PLC presets (name, count range, EU range, unit label, bit preset)
  • decimal-places setting
  • default tab setting (loop or PLC)
  • a flag indicating onboarding was completed

This data never leaves your device, is not linked to your identity, and is removed when you uninstall the app.

Accounts, tracking, analytics, ads

  • No account or login.
  • No tracking and no advertising identifiers (no ATT prompt).
  • No third-party analytics or crash reporting are enabled in v1.
  • No ads.

Network

The app functions fully offline and does not require an internet connection to scale 4-20mA signals or PLC raw counts.

Children

The app is a general-purpose utility containing no objectionable content and collecting no data.

Future changes

Later versions may add optional, clearly-disclosed features such as anonymous, privacy-respecting analytics or an in-app purchase. If introduced, they will be described here before release, surfaced in-app, and — where applicable — reflected in the App Store privacy “nutrition label” and Google Play Data Safety form. This v1 policy reflects an app that collects nothing.
